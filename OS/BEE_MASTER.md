# I. FOUNDATIONAL INTENT

The Book Extraction Machine exists to:

1.  Convert dense written knowledge into structurally encoded
    mechanisms.
2.  Preserve nuance without narrative bloat.
3.  Prevent hallucinated abstraction.
4.  Enforce epistemic discipline.
5.  Build a transferable primitive library for future modeling across
    domains.
6.  Serve as a long-horizon knowledge extractor for unknown future
    domains.

This is not a summarizer. This is not a note-taking system. This is not
a highlights engine.

It is a structural excavation apparatus.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# II. CORE PHILOSOPHY

A book is: - A compressed topology of interacting forces. - A
distribution of claims across time. - A mechanism stack hidden beneath
rhetoric. - A density field of incentives, thresholds, gradients,
feedback loops, and constraints.

Extraction means: - Decompressing topology. - Separating signal from
rhetorical ornament. - Identifying irreducible causal skeletons. -
Encoding those skeletons in transferable form.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# III. ONTOLOGICAL STACK

## SIGNAL (Atomic Capture Layer)

Signal is the smallest extractable meaningful unit from source.

Signal fields:

-   SIGNAL_ID
-   VERBATIM_TEXT (exact quoted text only when truly verbatim)
-   SOURCE_REFERENCE (page/section/anchor)
-   MINIMAL_PARAPHRASE
-   CONTEXT_TAG
-   CLAIM_TYPE (descriptive, prescriptive, probabilistic, structural)
-   CONFIDENCE_LEVEL

Signals are high volume. Signals are not canon. Signals are staging
inputs.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## KERNEL (Mechanism Candidate Layer)

Kernel is a clustered group of signals forming a provisional mechanism
hypothesis.

Kernel fields:

-   KERNEL_ID
-   MECHANISM_NAME
-   CORE_CAUSAL_STATEMENT
-   SUPPORTING_SIGNALS (3--7 SIGNAL_IDs)
-   INITIAL_VARIABLE_SET
-   PRELIMINARY_BOUNDARY
-   PRELIMINARY_BREAK_CONDITION
-   DOMAIN_CONTEXT

Kernel is a compression zone. Kernel is not yet irreducible.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## PRIMITIVE (Canonical Structural Unit --- SET IN STONE)

Primitive = irreducible structural extraction unit.

Primitive is admitted only through PAT (Primitive Admission Test).

Full Primitive Schema (unabridged):

1.  PRIMITIVE_ID
2.  MECHANISM_NAME
3.  ABSTRACT_STRUCTURE (2--5 lines)
4.  FORMAL_DESCRIPTION (equation, inequality, mapping, graph logic)
5.  PERCEPTUAL_DESCRIPTION (intuitive articulation)
6.  VARIABLE_LIST (typed, categorized: state, gradient, threshold,
    stochastic)
7.  RELATIONSHIP_MODEL (detailed directional dependencies)
8.  ASSUMPTIONS (explicit environmental and rationality assumptions)
9.  BOUNDARY_CONDITIONS (environmental constraints)
10. BREAK_CONDITIONS (collapse cases)
11. FAILURE_MODES (misapplication risks)
12. OBSERVABLE_SIGNALS (real-world detectability cues)
13. TRANSFER_MAP (cross-domain mapping lines)
14. DATA_ENCODING_TEMPLATE (node types, edge types, gradients,
    thresholds)
15. ISOMORPHISM_CLASS (family grouping)
16. DEPENDENCY_GRAPH_LINKS (which primitives rely on which)

Primitive is canon. Primitive is durable. Primitive is encodable.

# IV. FOUR-LAYER SYNTHESIS DOCTRINE (FULL EXPANSION)

## LAYER 1 --- STRUCTURAL SYNTHESIS

Purpose: Transform textual claims into formalizable causal mechanisms.

Responsibilities: - Extract variables. - Identify gradients. - Detect
threshold behaviors. - Identify asymmetries. - Capture feedback loops. -
Detect non-linearities. - Separate static from dynamic behavior.

Risks: - Over-abstraction. - Mechanism conflation. - Premature
compression.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## LAYER 2 --- NARRATIVE CONTINUITY

Purpose: Preserve structural context provided by author sequencing.

Responsibilities: - Track order of mechanism introduction. - Record
didactic scaffolding. - Capture example logic. - Identify rhetorical
emphasis. - Detect metaphorical framing that implies mechanism.

Risks: - Narrative inflation. - Romanticizing the text. - Confusing
example with mechanism.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## LAYER 3 --- EPISTEMIC INTEGRITY (Adversarial Constraint)

Purpose: Guard against structural hallucination.

Responsibilities: - Claim vs interpretation labeling. - Source
anchoring. - Scope limitation. - Explicit assumption declaration. -
Falsifiability identification. - Counterexample exploration.

Adversarial Direction: Against overreach. Against projection. Against
mythologizing.

Risks: - Over-cynicism. - Excessive fragmentation.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## LAYER 4 --- COHERENCE INTEGRATION

Purpose: Maintain global ontology stability.

Responsibilities: - Detect duplicate skeletons. - Identify structural
isomorphisms. - Resolve contradictory primitives. - Merge overlapping
mechanisms. - Map family classes (Pressure, Threshold, Feedback,
Asymmetry, etc.).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### LAYER 4C --- NAMELESS PRUNING (Disciplined Entropy)

Definition: Removal of structural redundancy and low-density constructs
to preserve energy flow within ontology.

Rules:

1.  Duplicate causal skeleton → merge.
2.  Redundant abstraction → remove.
3.  Weak break conditions → downgrade to Kernel.
4.  Non-transferable construct → label domain-bound.
5.  Excessive variable sprawl → split or prune.

Pruning is not compression. Pruning is selective elimination for
coherence.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# V. PRIMITIVE ADMISSION TEST (PAT) --- FULL FORM

A Kernel may be promoted only if:

1.  Irreducible without causal loss.
2.  Encodable as graph or equation.
3.  Break conditions observable in practice.
4.  Transferable or explicitly domain-bound.
5.  Survives isomorphism scan.
6.  Survives contradiction attempt.
7.  Survives adversarial reinterpretation test.

Failure at any stage → remains Kernel.
